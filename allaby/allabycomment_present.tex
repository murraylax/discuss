\frame
{
  \ft{Linear trends}
  \bi
  \item Linear upward trend corn crop yield may not be appropriate for 2016 forecast.
    \bi
    \item It has been nearly linear since 1940.
    \item Last 30 years has seen increased volatility.
    \item Crops dedicated to ethanol use different variety of corn.
    \ei
  \item Linear upward trend in ethanol yield not likely.
    \bi
    \item Pimental (1998), Graboski (2001): very different estimates for net energy balance.
    \item High demand for ethanol, high corn prices, should induce R\&D.
    \ei
  \ei
}

  \item Lacking economic explanations:
    \bi
    \item Why will the U.S. be demanding 35 billion gallons of ethanol?
    \item What economic incentives will Federal programs provide for ethanol usage and/or production?
    \item How will consumers and farmers react to these changing incentives?
